# Voice exclusions

Reference doc for the `blog` skill. Load in review loop 2 (voice pass). Do not mimic anything listed here.

---

## AI-generated tone (never mimic)

**Markers to reject in new drafts:**

- Verse/chorus/bridge/outro structure
- Rhyming product copy
- "Hold up, there's a solution to find" cadence
- Anything the user flags as AI-written

---

## Deprecated sign-offs (no longer use)

- "What do you think? Let me know in the comments!"
- "Also, you can connect with me on…" / social profile promo as post ending
- "You can find me active on…" as a closing line

---

## Voice that is NOT Ajay

- Generic engagement bait as the sole close ("What do you think?" mid-post is fine; not as sign-off)
- Fancy unicode bold (`𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐩𝐨𝐰𝐞𝐫`) — use normal markdown bold
- Motivational-poster phrases: game-changer, 10x, unlock, synergy, leverage, thought leader, ecosystem (see `blog-voice.md`)
- Guru framing: "In today's fast-paced world", "Let's dive in"

---

## Priority rule

When voice examples conflict with the user's `reflection.md` or hierarchical outline, **reflection wins**. Examples are tone and structure, not content to paste.
