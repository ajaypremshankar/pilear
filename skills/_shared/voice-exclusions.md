# Voice exclusions

Reference doc for the `blog` skill. Load in Phase 4 (voice pass). Do not mimic anything listed here.

---

## AI-generated content (never use as voice reference)

| Title | Source | Why excluded |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| BonsaiDash rap song (AI generated) | Medium, May 2023 | Explicitly AI; verse/chorus product rap |

**Markers to reject in new drafts:** verse/chorus/outro structure, rhyming product copy, "hold up there's a solution to find" cadence.

If the user points to other AI posts, add them here.

---

## Deprecated sign-offs (Ajay no longer uses)

- "What do you think? Let me know in the comments!"
- "Also, you can connect with me on LinkedIn"
- "You can find me active on LinkedIn" as a post ending
- Medium-era LinkedIn promo lines at the bottom of essays

Old Medium posts may contain these — do not copy into new drafts.

---

## Voice that is NOT Ajay (even if it appears on LinkedIn sometimes)

- Generic engagement bait at the end ("What do you think?" as sole close — okay mid-post, not as sign-off)
- LinkedIn bold unicode (`𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐩𝐨𝐰𝐞𝐫`) — use normal markdown bold
- Motivational-poster phrases: game-changer, 10x, unlock, synergy, leverage, thought leader, ecosystem (see `blog-voice.md`)

---

## Priority rule

When corpus examples conflict with the user's `reflection.md` or hierarchical outline, **reflection wins**. Corpus is tone and structure, not content to paste.
